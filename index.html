Welcome to St. Eanswythe's Primary School

Welcome to St Eanswythe’s Primary School, a happy and caring community with a strong Christian ethos, located in the heart of town. We take a holistic approach to education, ensuring every child achieves excellent academic progress while developing into a kind, respectful, and responsible citizen.

If you are looking for a school place in September 2027, please contact us to book a tour of our friendly school.

From Reception onwards, our dedicated team provides a supportive environment and a stimulating curriculum that sparks imagination. We strive to nurture each child's unique talents through rich experiences in sports, music, and the arts, alongside frequent school trips and a wide range of extracurricular clubs.

We are incredibly proud of our school and the well-being of the children in our care. We hope you enjoy exploring our website to learn more, and we warmly invite you to contact us to arrange a visit so you can see our vibrant school community in action.

I look forward to welcoming you.


Mrs Jacobs
Headteacher
